What the evidence says

Response is fast enough; booking handoff loses context

  • Observed: Median first human response is 11 minutes during the service window.
  • Observed: Three qualified Leads opened the booking route but only one retained the campaign reference.
  • Unknown: Two WhatsApp conversations were manually matched and cannot be provider-confirmed.
  • Workload: Owner 41 minutes · operator 96 minutes · 2 manual gaps.
  • Trust: No wrong publication; one opt-out was applied after human reconciliation.
