# Gate G0 Outreach Tool Research

**Reviewed:** 15 July 2026  
**Decision scope:** Operator tooling for founder-led Gate G0 research; not an application CRM/runtime decision  
**Recommendation:** Keep the accepted restricted Google Drive/Sheet as the canonical G0 workspace. Do not purchase or connect Kommo for Gate G0.

## 1. What the tool must do

The immediate job is small and evidence-heavy: manage 25–30 candidates, at least 10–20 owner interviews, five prototype tests, two workflow observations, five partner-readiness records, and a dated action queue for three operators. The tool must:

- preserve one studio/owner denominator record;
- support custom stages, required fields, notes, tasks, and simple forms;
- stop activity after an opt-out or decline;
- export usable CSV;
- avoid automated marketing and Meta/WhatsApp integrations;
- keep private identities outside Git and ordinary AI/chat tools; and
- avoid becoming a second source of truth during the initial one- to two-week evidence sprint; full Gate G0 remains evidence-driven and may take longer.

## 3. Why Kommo is the wrong shortcut now

Kommo is capable, but its strongest current features are the exact capabilities this project has deliberately excluded from the initial route:

- its Instagram integration connects an Instagram Business account, manages DMs in Kommo, and can automate comment/DM engagement;
- its WhatsApp Business integration is built on Meta's Cloud API and supports automated replies, templates, broadcasts, and campaigns;
- its pricing page promotes messenger integrations, email parsing, web forms, automated scheduling/follow-ups, and AI automation; and
- it requires at least a six-month paid term after the trial.

Using only Kommo's board and tasks would duplicate the already-approved Google Sheet while paying for features that must stay off. Using its messenger or Salesbot features would conflict with accepted OD-018/OD-019/OD-033 boundaries and would require a recorded scope/decision change plus current platform, privacy, access, and readiness evidence. The private tenant URL supplied by the founder was not accessed or changed during this research.

## 4. Recommended stack and fallback rule

### Gate G0 default

Use the [G0 operating pipeline](../discovery/G0_OUTREACH_OPERATING_PIPELINE.md):

1. Google Sheet is the private canonical register.
2. Google Form A captures only owner interest, minimal fit, permission, and availability.
3. Internal Forms or direct Sheet entry capture interviews, prototype tests, and workflow observations.
4. Humans send every Gmail, Instagram, WhatsApp, or other-platform message.
5. Calendar/Meet handles an agreed interview after the owner replies or opts in.
6. The local redacted checker calculates the first interview checkpoint.

### Reconsider a CRM only when

Re-evaluate Freshsales, Bigin, Airtable, or another tool only if the field team records a concrete blocker such as:

- the Sheet causes missed owner-approved follow-ups despite the action queue;
- three operators cannot maintain ownership or permissions safely;
- relational evidence becomes error-prone at the actual Gate G0 volume; or
- at least two design partners later evidence the same CRM handoff requirement described by OD-034.

Before adoption, run a synthetic-only trial: no real contact imports, no Meta/WhatsApp/email integration, no AI over private notes, no purchase, and no production connection. Score setup time, required fields, opt-out protection, role controls, CSV export/deletion, portability, subprocessor/AI terms, and total cost. Record the decision before migrating identities.

## 5. Privacy and readiness notes

The Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025 and phased enforcement timeline are now published by India's Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. This project already routes notices, consent/withdrawal, processor terms, retention, operator terms, signatures, invoicing, and payment handling to qualified review. A CRM trial is not a legal-readiness shortcut.

Kommo's current privacy policy says Client Data may be processed by subprocessors, including AI providers when AI features are enabled, and may be stored or transferred in the United States or other jurisdictions. Google likewise states that data outside eligible data-region coverage may be processed where Google or its subprocessors maintain facilities. Any real-data tool adoption therefore remains subject to the project's actual notice, minimization, access, retention, processor, cross-border, deletion, incident, and counsel-review requirements.
