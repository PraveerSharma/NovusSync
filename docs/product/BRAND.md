# NovusSync Brand

**Status:** Founder-selected product identity  
**Selected:** 17 July 2026

## Name

**NovusSync**

Pronunciation: `NO-vus-sync`

The name combines `novus` (new) with `sync` (coordinated execution). It supports a
business-agnostic product that connects campaigns, enquiries, approvals, work, and
measurable outcomes without tying the brand to one industry or channel.

## Product descriptor

> Approval-controlled marketing operations, from campaign to outcome.

## Product promise

> NovusSync helps businesses coordinate campaigns, respond to enquiries, and track
> real outcomes while authorized people retain control of external actions.

## Domain signal

On 17 July 2026, Verisign RDAP returned `404 Not Found` for `novussync.com`, which is
the normal signal that the `.com` registry had no active registration record at that
moment. This is not a reservation or guarantee; availability can change until the
founder registers the domain.

## Naming risk

`NovusSync` is already used as a PunnkFunnk smartwatch model in public retail listings.
That creates a search and potential trademark-confusion risk even though the product
category differs. The founder selected the name with this preliminary risk recorded.
Formal trademark clearance remains separate legal work.

## Usage

- Write the product name as `NovusSync` in customer-facing text.
- Use `novussync` for package names, identifiers, and domains.
- Use the package scope `@novussync/*`.
- Do not describe the name or domain as legally cleared, protected, or reserved.
