# Design-Partner Brief

**Status:** Recruitment discussion draft, not a contract, invoice, privacy notice, or promise of service.  
**Audience:** Eligible independent yoga-studio owners in Bengaluru Urban.  
**Before signature or payment:** Use counsel/accountant-approved participation, privacy, invoice/deposit, refund, and operator documents under OD-033.

## The problem we are testing

Instagram content, WhatsApp enquiries, trial-class booking, attendance, and campaign reporting often live in separate tools or people's memory. The result can be slow follow-up and weak evidence about which campaign produced an attended introductory trial.

We are testing a small, hands-on workflow that keeps the studio in control while making that path easier to operate and measure.

## What one 28-day cycle includes

- one introductory offer and one primary outcome: a qualified, attended trial class;
- four single-image Instagram content items and two carousels of up to five slides;
- a campaign-specific hosted landing/form as the primary Lead-entry route;
- a tracked click-to-WhatsApp route and a human-operated WhatsApp playbook;
- owner-approved campaign facts, content, qualification rules, and exact versions;
- use of the studio's existing booking system or a documented manual booking route;
- manual evidence linking campaign, enquiry, booking, and verified attendance where available;
- managed onboarding, quality checks, exception help, and a weekly evidence review; and
- a close-out summary with limitations and one proposed next experiment.

The campaign may run for up to 28 days. Any Instagram publication and WhatsApp message is reviewed and performed by an authorized human through the official account/app.

## What the studio controls

- business facts, prices, offers, claims, policies, and final content approval;
- Instagram and WhatsApp accounts, access, recovery, and human actions;
- qualification decisions, sensitive conversations, pause/takeover, and opt-outs;
- booking schedules, locations, instructors, capacity, payments, cancellations, and waitlists;
- attendance and enrollment/loss verification;
- advertising budget and any separately approved paid placement; and
- whether to continue into the next cycle.

No passwords are shared. Operators use individual authorized access. The product does not publish, message, book, or spend automatically in the initial pilot.

## What we need from a design partner

- an owner accountable for decisions and weekly review;
- intent to complete two 28-day cycles, with cancellation allowed before the next cycle;
- a usable four-week baseline for enquiries, response, booking, attendance, enrollment/loss, spend, and owner/staff effort where available;
- one professional Instagram account, one official WhatsApp Business number, and one workable booking route;
- approval to record minimized campaign/outcome evidence and manual work under the final privacy/commercial terms;
- timely review of facts, content, account readiness, capacity, and outcomes; and
- honest feedback about failures, workload, and value.

## First-five pricing

| Selected before activation | Price per 28-day cycle |
|---|---:|
| Up to 100 new Leads | INR 4,999 plus applicable tax |
| 101–200 new Leads | INR 6,999 plus applicable tax |

- INR 1,000 commitment deposit, fully credited to cycle one.
- No separate setup fee.
- Payment before each activation.
- Advertising and third-party booking/provider charges remain separate and customer-paid.
- No surprise per-Lead overage. Excess inbound evidence is retained, but volume can trigger owner handoff, capacity review, or a pause on new campaign admission.
- If our team cannot launch despite timely customer readiness, the unused cycle fee is refunded or fully credited at the customer's choice under the final approved terms.

## What is not promised

- No guaranteed number of Leads, bookings, attended trials, enrollments, or revenue.
- No claim that content or operation is automatically platform-compliant or legally compliant.
- No Meta API, automated Instagram publishing, automated WhatsApp, automated email, CRM replacement, or software-controlled advertising.
- No 24/7 human service. The planned pilot window is 10:00–20:00 `Asia/Kolkata` daily.
- No support for medical/therapeutic, child-directed, or other regulated claims/workflows.

## How selection works

1. Owner interview and eligibility check.
2. Baseline, account, booking, outcome, capacity, and support-readiness review.
3. Review of the clickable prototype and proposed workflow.
4. Counsel/accountant-approved participation, privacy, deposit/invoice, and refund documents.
5. Signed two-cycle participation commitment.
6. Deposit or first-cycle payment through the approved route.

Selection is limited to five design partners. Interest alone does not reserve a place, and no live campaign starts until the later legal, privacy, account, provider, operator, and production-readiness gates pass.

## Suggested next conversation

Review one recent campaign together using anonymous or studio-controlled evidence: the offer, Instagram post, enquiry route, first response, follow-up, booking, attendance, outcome, and time spent. That shows whether the pilot fits without asking the studio to trust a slide deck.
