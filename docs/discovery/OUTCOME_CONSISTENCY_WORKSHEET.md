# Gate G0 Blind Outcome-Consistency Worksheet

**Status:** Ready for independent human review; no classifications recorded  
**Gate row:** `G0-09`  
**Rule under review:** [`G0-OUTCOME-v1`](OUTCOME_DEFINITION_AND_EXAMPLES.md)  
**Evidence boundary:** This worksheet prepares a review. It does not pass `G0-09` until the founder and a named product-lead reviewer complete the exercise independently and the comparison record is signed.

## 1. Reviewer protocol

1. Assign two human reviewers: the founder and one named product lead who understands the product boundary.
2. Each reviewer reads only sections 1–9 of the [outcome rule](OUTCOME_DEFINITION_AND_EXAMPLES.md#1-plain-language-rule). Do not open its classification examples or view the other reviewer's answers.
3. Each reviewer independently completes **Set A** in a separate private copy. Record only scenario IDs and de-identified classifications in the repository; names/signatures remain in the restricted evidence store.
4. Lock both responses before comparing them.
5. Compare the count, state, and reason for every scenario against each other and the written rule.
6. If any material difference exists, clarify the rule, create a new rule version, and independently repeat with **Set B**. Do not coach a reviewer toward agreement.
7. Record the final comparison in section 4 and in the canonical rule review record. Only then may `G0-09` be considered for `PASS`.

Anyone who has already viewed a scenario's expected answer cannot supply an independent classification for that scenario. An AI or synthetic rehearsal may test the worksheet but does not replace either named human reviewer.

## 2. Set A — first independent attempt

### `BLIND-A01` — inclusive time boundaries

A hosted-form Lead is captured on 29 July for a campaign that closes on 30 July. The Lead satisfies every criterion in the approved rule, books on 5 August, attends the linked introductory trial on 13 August, and the authorized owner verifies the attendance. Source is known and there is no duplicate or dispute.

### `BLIND-A02` — required answer remains unknown

A hosted-form Lead has known campaign attribution and wants the introductory offer, but one mandatory serviceable-location criterion remains unanswered. The Lead books and the authorized owner verifies an in-window attendance. No authorized qualification override or later answer exists.

### `BLIND-A03` — two records for one appointment

One qualified campaign Lead books and attends one in-window introductory trial. The same provider appointment identifier appears once in a provider export and once in an authorized manual reconciliation. Reviewers confirm both records describe the same appointment and there is no conflicting state.
