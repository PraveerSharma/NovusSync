## 7. Calculation and stop rules

- Repeated-pain rate = included unique eligible studio-owner records with behavioral evidence of **both** pains / all included unique eligible studio-owner records. One `studio_code` represents one owner-operated business across its locations and may enter the denominator only once.
- A repeat/follow-up row may remain in the coded packet only with an explicit `denominator_exclusion_reason`; exclusions are reported and never remove or replace the first included eligible `NO` response. Report numerator, denominator, and every exclusion.
- Finding stability is not a feeling: after interview 10, record whether each new interview adds a material pain, workflow, segment, objection, or contradiction.
- Do not average away a privacy, account-access, platform-terms, regulated-claim, inability-to-verify-attendance, or payment contradiction.
- Fail G0 or reopen the relevant decision if the five-partner Bengaluru cohort cannot be recruited, fewer than 70% show both pains, the workflow cannot produce governed attendance evidence, no workable manual channel/booking route exists, or paid commitment cannot be obtained.
- Resolve, explicitly accept with a bounded mitigation, or reject every material contradiction. “We will solve it later” is not a disposition.
